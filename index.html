SkillHood

A Hyperlocal Time Banking and Skill Exchange Platform

This project proposes a hyperlocal community platform where neighbors can exchange skills and time instead of money. Users can teach skills such as music or language while receiving help in gardening, tutoring, or technical tasks. The system includes a time banking mechanism, reputation system, and smart matching algorithm to strengthen community bonds and increase accessibility to services.

About the Project

Problem Statement

Modern communities face challenges in building meaningful connections and accessing services. Traditional monetary transactions create barriers for those with limited financial resources, while valuable skills and knowledge within neighborhoods remain underutilized. There is a need for a system that facilitates skill exchange and strengthens community bonds without relying solely on financial transactions.

Proposed Solution

SkillHood addresses these challenges by creating a hyperlocal platform that enables neighbors to exchange skills and time through a time banking system. The platform uses intelligent matching algorithms to connect community members based on their skills, needs, and availability, fostering mutual support and strengthening neighborhood relationships.

Technical Scope

  • Web & Mobile Application
  • Matching Algorithm
  • Scheduling System
  • Reputation & Review System
  • Time Banking Infrastructure
  • Community Management Dashboard

Social Impact

SkillHood aims to strengthen neighborhood relationships by facilitating meaningful connections between community members. The platform increases accessibility to services for people with limited financial resources, encourages volunteerism and civic engagement, and creates a sustainable model for community support that benefits all participants.

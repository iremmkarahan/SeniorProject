The Vision

Community Service

SkillSwap helps people access services they can't afford by leveraging the collective knowledge of their neighborhood.

Time Banking

Our unique time banking feature tracks community service hours. An hour of your skill equals an hour of someone else's.

Reputation System

Every swap builds trust. Our review and rating system ensures quality and creates a safe space for community exchanges.
